Week 14 Injury Report

Team Name Player Name Injury Status News
Arizona Cardinals Andre Ellington Out Ellington is set to return from his turf toe injury in week 15.
Denver Broncos Ronnie Hillman Questionable Hillman is suffering from a foot injury but is still likely to play. Look for him to get in a full practice on Friday to be sure of his status.
Denver Broncos C.J. Anderson Questionable Anderson re-aggravated the same ankle injury he had earlier in the season and at this point is a game time decision.
New Orleans Saints Mark Ingram IR Ingram was placed on season ending IR on Wednesday with a torn rotator cuff.
Philadelphia Eagles Ryan Mathews Probable Mathews is set to return this week after missing the past few weeks with a concussion.
Seattle Seahawks Marshawn Lynch Out Pete Carrol announced this week that Lynch's recovery has been slow and will likely miss a few more weeks after having core muscle surgery in mid-November.
Washington Redskins Chris Thompson Doubtful After suffering a shoulder injury in Week 13 Thompson looks unlikely to play this week.
