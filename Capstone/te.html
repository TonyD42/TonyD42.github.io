Week 14 Injury Report

Tight Ends

Team Name Player Name Injury Status News
Atlanta Falcons Jacob Tamme Questionable Tamme has been limited in practice with a shoulder injury but looks likely to play.
Baltimore Ravens Crockett Gilmore Questionable Although the Raven's were optimistic Gilmore would return from his back injury earlier in the week he has remained limited in practice and looks unlikely to play.
Buffalo Bills Charles Clay Probable While remaining limited in practice with a knee injury it looks like Clay is going to suit up Sunday for his match-up against the Eagles.
Chicago Bears Martellus Bennett IR Martellus Bennett is done for the season with a rib injury.
Chicago Bears Zach Miller Probable Miller is limited with a rib injury but is likely to play on Sunday.
Cincinatti Bengals Tyler Eifert Questionable Eifert practiced in full on Thursday and looks to play in a good matchup Sunday against the Steelers.
Cleveland Browns Gary Barnidge Questionable Barnidge has been limited with an ankle injury but feels he will be ready for Sunday.
Denver Broncos Vernon Davis Questionable Davis returned to practice on Thursday and barring any symptoms his concussion should not keep him out of Sunday's match-up.
Denver Broncos Owen Daniels Probable The veteran has taken it easy this week in practice with a sore knee but will suit up on Sunday.
New England Patriots Scott Chandler Questionable Chandler has been limited all week with a knee injury but is likely to play this week.
New England Patriots Rob Gronkowski Questionable Gronk returned to practice in a limited fashion with a large brace on his injured knee. While he is day to day do not look for him to play this weekend.
Pittsburgh Steelers Heath Miller Questionable Miller hasn't played since he exited the game in Seattle with a rib injury two weeks ago, but he hopes to take the field this week after getting in some limited practice this week. Look for him to be a game time decision.
San Francisco 49ers Vance McDonald Doubtful McDonald is still in the concussion protocol and as such is unlikely to take the field this week.
